# TrueSpend Browser Extension - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [DATE]

## Introduction

TrueSpend ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the TrueSpend browser extension.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Authentication Information
- **Google Account Data:** When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture from Google OAuth.
- **Session Tokens:** We store authentication tokens locally in your browser to keep you signed in.
- **User ID:** A unique identifier assigned to your account for data association.

### 2. Financial Data
- **Budget Information:** Budget categories, limits, periods, and spending thresholds you create.
- **Transaction Data:** Transaction amounts, categories, merchants, and timestamps.
- **Geofence Data:** Location-based budget zones and their parameters.

### 3. Browsing Activity (Limited)
- **Merchant Detection:** When you visit e-commerce websites, we detect the merchant name and product prices to provide budget alerts.
- **Visited Domains:** Only for sites where merchant detection is active (e.g., amazon.com, walmart.com).
- **No Full Browsing History:** We do NOT track all websites you visit, only supported merchant sites.

### 4. Location Data (Optional)
- **Nearby Deals:** If you enable location-based features, we access your approximate location to find nearby deals.
- **Geofence Triggers:** Location data used to trigger budget alerts when entering budget zones.
- **User Control:** Location access can be disabled at any time in extension settings.

### 5. Usage Analytics (Optional)
- **Telemetry Events:** Extension usage metrics (e.g., popup opened, settings changed, merchant detected).
- **Error Logs:** Crash reports and error messages for debugging.
- **Feature Flags:** Which experimental features you're enrolled in.
- **Opt-Out:** Telemetry can be disabled in extension settings.

### 6. Device Information
- **Browser Type:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or other browser.
- **Extension Version:** Current version of the extension installed.
- **Operating System:** Windows, macOS, Linux, etc.

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Functionality
1. **Budget Tracking:** Calculate spending against your budgets in real-time.
2. **Merchant Detection:** Identify when you're shopping on supported sites.
3. **Alerts & Notifications:** Notify you when approaching or exceeding budget limits.
4. **Deal Discovery:** Alert you to relevant deals near your location (if enabled).
5. **Sync Across Devices:** Keep your budgets and settings synchronized.

### Product Improvement
1. **Analytics:** Understand how users interact with the extension (anonymous).
2. **Bug Fixes:** Identify and resolve technical issues.
3. **Feature Development:** Determine which features are most valuable.

### Legal & Security
1. **Fraud Prevention:** Detect and prevent unauthorized access.
2. **Compliance:** Meet legal and regulatory requirements.
3. **Security Monitoring:** Protect against malicious activity.

## Data Storage & Security

### Local Storage
- **Browser Storage:** Authentication tokens, settings, and cached budgets stored locally in your browser.
- **No Server Storage of Tokens:** Session tokens never leave your device except when authenticating with our API.

### Cloud Storage (Supabase)
- **Encrypted in Transit:** All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS 1.3.
- **Encrypted at Rest:** Database encryption for all stored data.
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access with row-level security policies.
- **Backup & Recovery:** Regular automated backups with point-in-time recovery.

### Data Retention
- **Active Users:** Data retained while your account is active.
- **Inactive Users:** Unverified accounts deleted after 30 days.
- **Account Deletion:** All data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
- **Telemetry Logs:** Deleted after 90 days.
