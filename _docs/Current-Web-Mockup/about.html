About TrueSpend

Built by people who hate
leaving money on the table.

The average household loses $487 a year in unclaimed rewards because nobody can remember which card earns what. We thought that was a job worth solving — quietly, privately, and without a single intrusive popup.

Founded 2024
Toronto · New York
Our mission

Make every household $500 a year richer without changing anything they spend.

We're not here to sell you another card, push you to a new bank, or shame your spending. Your money is yours. Your habits are yours. We're just the quiet assistant making sure that — when you do spend — the swipe pays you the most it possibly can.

That's it. That's the whole product.

What we won't compromise on

Three principles. Non-negotiable.

If a feature can't be shipped without bending one of these, the feature doesn't ship.

01

Privacy first, always

Your transactions, location, and reward history never leave your phone. We don't sell data. We don't run ads. We never will.

02

Every dollar is earned

If a tile shows you a number, we can show you the maths behind it. No "estimated savings" sleight of hand.

03

Quiet by default

Every push notification is worth $1 or more. Every dashboard tile answers "so what should I do?" If it's noise, we delete it.

How we got here

One missed Amex offer at Whole Foods, two years of building.

2024 Q1

Aanya tries six rewards-tracking apps. None of them tell her which card to use. She sketches the first wireframe on a Costco receipt.

2024 Q3

Marcus joins. First closed alpha launches with 40 hand-picked users. Average recovered rewards: $387 in 90 days.

2025 Q2

Internal rewards database hits 99% credit card coverage. Geofence engine ships. Notification fatigue rules go live.

2026

Public waitlist opens. 2,400+ sign-ups in eight weeks. Premium pricing locked at $5/month — and we mean it.
