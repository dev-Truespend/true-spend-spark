About

We're building the privacy-first finance app.

Most finance apps treat your data like a product. We treat it like a responsibility. TrueSpend is built around three principles: your data stays yours, the AI works for you (not for advertisers), and the interface gets out of the way.

🎯

Our mission

Help people understand and direct their spending without giving up control of their data.

📅

Founded

2025 · Built across 16 phases / 51 weeks. Solo-engineered with AI assistance.

🌍

Where
