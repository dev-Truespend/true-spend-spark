Frequently asked

The questions before the questions.

Is TrueSpend a bank or a credit card?

Neither. We don't issue cards, we don't hold money. TrueSpend is the assistant that helps you use the cards you already have, better.

How do you make money on the Free tier?

We don't, on the Free tier. Premium subscriptions fund the company. We will never sell your data — see our privacy commitment.

Will TrueSpend hurt my credit score?

No. We read transactions through a secure Plaid connection — there is no credit pull and no impact on your score.

What data do you actually see?

Credit card transactions for the cards you connect (24 months of history), the cards' reward grids from our internal database, and your GPS while the app is open. No income, no checking balance, no bank ties.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Premium is month-to-month with no annual contract. Cancel from settings, no email required.

Why is the tagline "Maximise every swipe"?

Because every contactless tap is a swipe — and every swipe is a chance to be on the right card. We say it both ways: the British spelling because our founders learned to count pennies before dollars.
