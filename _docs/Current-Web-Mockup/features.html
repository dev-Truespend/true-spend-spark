Core features

Everything in TrueSpend

A plain-English view of what the product does across the mobile app, browser extension, and website.

💳

Best card recommendation

TrueSpend tells you which credit card is likely to earn the most rewards for a purchase.

🏬

Store and website helper

Use location for physical stores or a website link for online shopping.

🛒

Category choice

For big stores that sell many things, choose what you are buying so the advice is accurate.

📈

Missed rewards

See where a different card could have earned more cash back or points.

🔗

Card linking

Connect cards through Plaid or add them manually by issuer and card name.

🧾

Receipt capture

Take a receipt photo and review the merchant, amount, date, and items.

🎯

Budgets

Set spending limits by category and get alerts before you go over.

🔔

Notifications

Get reminders for better card choices, budget warnings, receipts, and sync issues.

📱

Three product phases

First mobile app, then browser extension, then website for larger-screen management.

Mobile app (iOS & Android) shipping first, followed by browser extension and website.
