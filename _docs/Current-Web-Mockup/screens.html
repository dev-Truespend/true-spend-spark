Core features

What a normal user gets

These are the main product promises written in plain language.

💳

Know which card to use

When you are shopping, TrueSpend tells you which of your cards gives the best rewards for that purchase.

🏬

Works in stores and online

Use your location for nearby stores, or paste a website link when shopping online.

🛒

Handles stores with many categories

For places like Target or Walmart, the app asks what you are buying before recommending a card.

📈

Shows missed rewards

See where you used the wrong card and how much cash back or points you could have earned.

🧾

Saves receipts

Take a photo of a receipt and TrueSpend reads the merchant, amount, date, and items for you.

🔔

Sends useful alerts

Get reminders for better card choices, budget limits, receipt issues, and account sync problems.

🎯

Tracks budgets

Set spending limits by category and get warned before you go over.

🔒

Gives control over data

Export your information, delete your account data, and manage how sync works.

Three phases

Product rollout

1

Mobile app

First release for iOS and Android. It covers card recommendations, card linking, transactions, insights, notifications, privacy, and settings.

2

Browser extension

Second release for online shopping. It helps pick the best card while browsing merchant websites.

3

Website

Third release for desktop use. It gives users larger screens for reports, budgets, receipts, cards, and account management.
